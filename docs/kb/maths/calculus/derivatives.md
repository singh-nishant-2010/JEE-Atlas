# Derivatives

Derivative represents rate of change.

dy/dx = lim h→0 (f(x+h) − f(x))/h

## Standard Derivatives

d/dx (xⁿ) = nxⁿ⁻¹

d/dx (sin x) = cos x

d/dx (cos x) = −sin x

d/dx (tan x) = sec²x

## Product Rule

d(uv)/dx = u dv/dx + v du/dx

## Chain Rule

dy/dx = dy/du × du/dx

## Applications

- Slope of tangent
- Maxima & minima
- Velocity & acceleration
