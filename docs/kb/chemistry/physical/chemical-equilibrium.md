# Chemical Equilibrium

## Law of Mass Action

For reaction:

aA + bB ⇌ cC + dD

K = [C]^c[D]^d / [A]^a[B]^b

## Types of Equilibrium

1. Homogeneous
2. Heterogeneous

## Le Chatelier Principle

System shifts to counteract external change.

Factors:

- Concentration
- Temperature
- Pressure

## Equilibrium Constant

Kc → concentration  
Kp → pressure

Relation:

Kp = Kc(RT)^(Δn)
