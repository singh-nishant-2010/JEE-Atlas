# Chemical Bonding

## Types of Bonds

- Ionic
- Covalent
- Coordinate

## VSEPR Theory

Predicts shape of molecules.

Examples:

CH4 → tetrahedral

NH3 → trigonal pyramidal

H2O → bent

## Hybridization

sp → linear  
sp² → trigonal planar  
sp³ → tetrahedral
