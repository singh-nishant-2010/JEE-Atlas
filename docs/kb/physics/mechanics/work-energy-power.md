# Work Energy Power

## Work

W = F·s·cosθ

Unit: Joule

## Kinetic Energy

K = ½mv²

## Work Energy Theorem

Work done by net force equals change in kinetic energy.

W = ΔK

## Potential Energy

Gravitational:

U = mgh

Elastic:

U = ½kx²

## Power

P = dW/dt

Average Power:

P = W/t

Instantaneous Power:

P = F·v

## Conservative Forces

- Work independent of path
- Potential energy defined

Examples:
- Gravitational
- Spring force

## Mechanical Energy

E = K + U

Conserved if only conservative forces act.
