# English Tenses

## Present Tense

Simple Present

Example:

She writes a letter.

## Past Tense

Simple Past

She wrote a letter.

## Future Tense

She will write a letter.

## Important Rules

Use present tense for universal truths.

Example:

The sun rises in the east.
